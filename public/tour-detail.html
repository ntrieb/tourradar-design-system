About this tour

Embark on this Classic Europe tour and explore top European destinations! Cruise Amsterdam's canals, and Germany's Rhine River. Capture iconic sites like London's Buckingham Palace, Italy's Leaning Tower of Pisa, and Paris' Eiffel Tower. Savour Tuscan delights, German brews, and Belgian waffles. Summit the Swiss Alps for breathtaking vistas. Revel in free time in Paris, Rome, Amsterdam, and the Swiss Alps. This fast-paced adventure ensures no highlights are missed.

Map

Itinerary

1
Start of tour in London
Lunch

Welcome to London, the starting point of this Classic Europe tour! This morning, arrive at the hotel and meet up with the tour leader and tour group. Begin the European adventure with a London city tour, checking off top landmarks before indulging in a classic British roast lunch at a local pub.

Experiences:

  • Explore London's top sights with your tour leader
  • Enjoy a roast lunch at a traditional British Pub
2
London - Paris
BreakfastDinner

This morning, the tour travels to Paris! Cross the English Channel and travel through to the French capital. Upon arrival, embark on a driving tour of the "City of Lights," featuring iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Arc de Triomphe, and more!

Experiences:

  • See the highlights of Paris on a driving tour!
3
Paris: Free Day
Breakfast

Parisian adventures await! Opt for independent exploration today, soaking up the city's charm, or join the jam-packed Paris+ excursion for a full day of guided discovery and activities. See iconic landmarks like the Arc de Triomphe and Champs-Elysees.

4
Paris - Burgundy - Swiss Alps
BreakfastDinner

Au revoir Paris! Today, savour the charm of small-town France with a lunch stop in Burgundy. Explore the town of Beaune, visit the beautiful Hospices de Beaune and sample renowned regional wines. Then, embark on a picturesque drive across the border into Switzerland.

Experiences:

  • Visit Beaune; wine capital of Burgundy
  • Marvel at the views on a scenic Swiss mountain drive
5
Swiss Alps: Free Day
Breakfast

Enjoy your free day breathing in the crisp Alpine air. Experience a guaranteed snow day at Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe," where snow covers the landscape year-round! Join the optional full-day excursion to this spectacular glacier which offers breathtaking views.

6
Swiss Alps - Pisa - Tuscany
Breakfast

Today, the tour trades snow-capped peaks for rolling Tuscan hills as it travels towards Italy. The first stop is the world-famous and UNESCO World Heritage Site of the Leaning Tower of Pisa.

Experiences:

  • Enjoy a photo stop at the Leaning Tower of Pisa
7
Tuscany - Florence - Rome
BreakfastDinner

The journey continues through the beautiful Tuscan region today with the first stop being Florence. Embark on a guided tour of this enchanting Renaissance city renowned for its art and history.

Experiences:

  • Discover Florence; the 'Jewel of the Renaissance'
8
Rome: Free Day
Breakfast

Get ready for a free day in Rome! Today offers the opportunity to immerse yourself in Rome's ancient history, rich culture, and delectable cuisine. Consider joining a locally guided tour to explore the Colosseum and Roman Forum.

9
Rome - Venice
Breakfast

Experience the incredible diversity of the Italian landscape as the tour ventures onward to Venice! This unique city spans across 118 islands and is famous for its canals, gondolas, Venetian masks, piazzas, art, and traditional crafts.

Experiences:

  • Explore Venice, Italy's floating city
10
Venice - Innsbruck - Munich
BreakfastDinner

Farewell, Italy, Guten Tag Germany! Today, the tour journeys to Germany's vibrant capital of the Bavaria region, Munich. En route, stop in Innsbruck, Austria, and embark on an orientation tour.

Experiences:

  • Visit the captivating Austrian city of Innsbruck
  • Discover Munich on an orientation tour
  • Enjoy a German meal in one of Munich's historic restaurants
11
Munich - Rhine Valley
BreakfastDinner

Continue the German adventure this morning by heading to the enchanting Rhine Valley. Arrive in one of the most picturesque areas along the Rhine River and embark on a cruise along its waters.

Experiences:

  • Enjoy a Rhine River Cruise
12
Rhine Valley - Amsterdam
BreakfastDinner

Say "Auf Wiedersehen" to Germany this morning as the tour heads towards the Netherlands! Pause in the Dutch countryside to learn about some of the country's renowned local products.

Experiences:

  • Enjoy an Amsterdam canal cruise
13
Amsterdam - Bruges - London
Breakfast

The tour continues to London today! The first stop is the medieval city of Bruges, in Belgium. Enjoy free time in the city to wander cobblestone streets, admire historic architecture, sample Belgian chocolate, and indulge in Belgian waffles!

Experiences:

  • Visit Bruges; a medieval Belgian city
14
End of tour in London
Breakfast

After two incredible weeks spent exploring Europe, the tour comes to an end this morning. Gather with the group for breakfast at the hotel, where you can bid farewell to your fellow travellers and new friends.
